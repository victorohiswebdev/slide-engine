## Image Specifications

- **Aspect ratio**: Landscape (16:9 minimum)
- **Resolution**: At least 1200px wide (slide canvas is 1920×1080)
- **Format**: `.jpg` preferred for smaller file size
- **Tone**: Natural, warm, professional. Avoid overly saturated/stocky looks.

## Slide-by-Slide Guide

### Slide 1 — 3 stacked images on the right
- All three need rounded corners (handled by CSS)
- Should tell a visual story: problem condition → manual effort → infrastructure gap

### Slide 4 — Watermark background
- Shown at 6% opacity as a subtle backdrop
- A calm, wide shot of farmland works best
- Avoid images with heavy texture — the low opacity means simple compositions look better
